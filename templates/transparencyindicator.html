Details

Timeliness

This is calculated using the assessments made on the frequency and timelag pages. Each assessment is given a numerical score:

Frequency assessments
Frequency assessment Score
Monthly 4
Quarterly 3
Six-Monthly 2
Annual 1
Less than Annual 0
Time lag assessments
Time lag assessment Score
One month 4
A quarter 3
Six months 2
One year 1
More than one year 0

This total value is then divided by 8 (i.e. the maximum possible score for both the frequency and time lag assessments). The result is then expressed as a percentage.

Forward looking

The mean average percentage of current activities with budgets (2015-16) for this publisher. The component values are displayed on the forward looking page.

Comprehensive

The mean average of comprehensiveness average values for core, financials and value-added. The core average has a double-weighting for this calculation.

Score

The mean average of the three values above.

Coverage

The value expressed in the Coverage tab. (N.B. In practice the coverage value for each publisher has been manually set at 100% for consultation purposes. When sufficient data on each publisher's total spend has been gathered, a calculation for the percentage of spending reported to IATI can be calculated.)

Coverage-adjusted score

The score is multiplied by the coverage percentage to create a new score adjusted for the amount of data published to IATI vs. total spending.
